Frequently Asked Questions

What is MBTI Media?
MBTI Media is an app that recommends videos based on your Myers-Briggs personality type and current activity. It helps you find content that resonates with your cognitive preferences.
How does the recommendation system work?
Our algorithm matches YouTube content to your MBTI personality type and chosen activity. Each personality type processes information differently, so we tailor recommendations to your unique preferences.
What are the Premium features?
Premium subscribers can access the "Load 5 More" feature to get additional video recommendations and save videos to their Favorites list for easy access later.
How does the free trial work?
New users automatically receive a 7-day free trial with access to all Premium features. After the trial period, you can subscribe for $1/month to continue enjoying Premium features.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions, find MBTI Media, and tap Cancel Subscription.
The app shows "Quota exceeded" - what does this mean?
This means we've reached our daily limit for YouTube API requests. The app will automatically use cached recommendations or switch to offline mode. This limit typically resets within 24 hours.
How do I change my MBTI type in the app?
You can change your MBTI type anytime by tapping your current type in the top section of the home screen and selecting a different one from the dropdown menu.
Can I use the app offline?
The app requires an internet connection to fetch new recommendations. However, your Favorites list is stored locally and can be accessed offline.
I don't know my MBTI type - how can I find it?
If you don't know your MBTI type, we recommend taking an online assessment. Several free tests are available online, such as 16Personalities.com or MBTIonline.com.

Privacy & Data

What data does the app collect?
We collect minimal data - only your selected MBTI type, chosen activities, and subscription status. We do not track personal information or browsing history.
Where are my favorites stored?
Your favorites are stored locally on your device. We don't sync this data to the cloud or across devices at this time.
